Exponential Distribution
------------------------
Time until next event.

X~Exp(λ)

Example case:
    - Time until the next train at a station.
    - Time until the next phone call at a call center.

Assumes:
    - The events occur with a constant average rate
    - The events are independent

Parameters:
    λ : The constant average rate (expected number of events per unit time)

Expectation and Variance:
    E(X) = 1 / λ
    Var(X) = 1 / λ^2

Probability Density Function (PDF):
    f(x) = λ * e^(-λ * x), for x ≥ 0

Cumulative Distribution Function (CDF):
    F(x) = ∫ f(x) dx
         = 1 - e^(-λ * x), for x ≥ 0
