Geometric Distribution
----------------------
Number of trials until the 1st "success".

X~Geo(p)

Example case:
    - Number of coin flips until a head (p = 1/2)
    - Number of dice rolls until a 6 (p = 1/6)

Assumes:
    - A trial is either a success or failure
    - Fixed probability of success
    - Trials are identical and independent

Parameters:
    p: The probability of a success

Expectation and Variance:
    E(X) = 1 / p
    Var(X) = (1 - p) / (p^2)

Probability Mass Function:
    P(X = r) = (1 - p)^(r - 1) * p
