Normal Distribution
-------------------
A common, naturally occurring distribution.

X~N(μ,σ²)

Example case:
    - Heights in the population.
    - Weight of newborn babies.

Assumes:
    - Data is continuous
    - Distribution is symmetric around the mean

Parameters:
    μ - Population mean
    σ² - Population variance

Expectation and Variance:
    E(X) = μ
    Var(X) = σ²

Probability Density Function (PDF):
    f(x) = (1 / (σ * sqrt(2 * π))) * (e^(-0.5 * ((x-μ) / σ)^2))
    Where sqrt(2 * π) is the square root of 2 * π
